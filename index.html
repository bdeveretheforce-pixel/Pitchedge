Straight Bets — Hunt Value
Goal: find plus money on high-probability outcomes. A +128 line that hits 55% of the time is pure edge. These picks are evaluated on whether the price is wrong relative to actual probability. You only need to be right a little more than break-even to profit long term.
Parlays — Stack Probability
Goal: combine high-probability legs, not high-payout legs. Three -150 legs in a parlay hits ~29% of the time and pays ~+275. Three +110 legs hits ~12% and pays ~+750. The first is far better EV if each leg is genuinely 60% likely. Parlay legs are chosen for probability to hit, not odds appeal.
